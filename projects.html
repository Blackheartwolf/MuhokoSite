Muhokian Projects

Hackathon

Developers at Muhoko Take 3rd Place



The Developers at Muhoko Had Taken Part in the 2020 Covid-19 Hackathon And Placed 3rd,winning a cash prize of N$15 000.00. They had developed an e-commerce website called HoverMarket.This website would serve as a platform for informal vendors to sell their products and for taxi drivers to act as delivery guys to earn an extra few dollars as a side hustle.

Web Development

Muhokian Web Developers


The Web Developers at Muhoko strive to deliver only the best websites to their clients. They have already developed sites for many others and are still in the process of delivering to current and future clients.
Junior Muhokians also gain the advantage of being trained by the seniors so that the development circle keeps on growing within Namibia.
